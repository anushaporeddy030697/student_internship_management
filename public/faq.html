Frequenty Asked Questions

What is this service?

This service is mainly focussed to connect students and companies in order to provide internship opportunities.

Is this service free ?

Yes, this service is absolutely free.

Can I apply to multiple companies?

Yes, You can apply to multiple companies.

Is there any limitation for number of applications?

No, There is no such limitations as long as you feel that the job description suits you. You are good to go for as many applications as you want.

Who audits these applications?

There is an admin to control any fake job offers.

who takes final desicion for job offer?

Job offering company is the final desicion maker here.
