About page

Student internship Management system is a dynamic way to manage students internships and their lifecycle.

There are seperate logins for students, companies and admins, where students register and apply for internships and check the application status.

Companies post jobs which will be broadcasted all over the students and get prospective internship applicants through this portal.

Admin has priviliged rights to control the activites.

To sum up this portal leverages great automation in job applications and help to reduce the efforts of each party.
